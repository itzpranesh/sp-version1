How to live a profound life?

Is there any blueprint for life? Is there a perfect path for success? Is there a map for happiness? Is there a clear path to live a profound life?

No. There is no path to my destination. It doesn’t mean that I can not reach my destination. I can. But I have to create a map of my own. Someone may have a same destination as mine but I should not to follow them. I must create a new path because my values and desires are different from others. I don’t worry thinking about "How-to".

I know what is needed and what I want in my life. Therefore the path is a natural cause. I do what it takes to reach my destination. I don't look up to others. Yes i may make some mistakes but that is ok. I can correct it.

  • I just take the next step.
  • And I do what makes sense to me in the long run.

Do not look at others. Create what is needed (for you or others or both). Don’t waste your time thinking on the "how to". Just take the next step.
Just do it.
