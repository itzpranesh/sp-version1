What I want in life?

Answering this question is hard.
I want a simple and happy life. I need to earn some money to pay my bills and to fulfil my small desires. That's it. If I take care of this money part alone, my need is over (hmm... may be for just now?). I have lovely people around me (my love and my family). And that is all I wanted.

  1. Earn some money to make my life easier.
  2. Being with my family.

I already have the second part. I just want to make some money.

But, this is not the case. As the time and situation changes 'what I want?' also changes. Sometimes situation around me may demand more. I'm not talking about someone's influence on me. Sometimes it is a requirement for me to do more just for the survival (medical expenses, debt etc).

I should be able to differentiate from the two:

1. What I want?

Answer for this question did not come just like that. It is an evolving process. My desires was changing time to time. I think this is an endless evolving process. Take this two steps:

Step 1: Right now what I want in my life?
Step 2: Make it happen.

2. What the situation wants from me?

Ask a sub question. 'Is there a way to escape or avoid this situation?'
If yes, avoid it.
If no, make it happen.
